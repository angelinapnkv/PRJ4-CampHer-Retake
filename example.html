Marit, 29 – Utrecht, NL

I’ve always loved the idea of freedom that comes with vanlife—just me, the road, and nature. But the fear of being alone, especially as a woman, kept holding me back... CampHer changed everything. From campsite tips to secure locking advice, it gave me the confidence to follow my dream. Since I started solo camping my mental health has skyrocketed and I feel way more confident in myself. Also, thanks to CampHer and their wonderful community I have managed to find friends I am confident to say will stay for life. It's amazing how much my life has changed.

Sofia, 24 – Breda, NL

Blogs and Insta posts never really spoke to my experience—until Campher. The real stories, the honest advice, the mindset focus... Campher helped me believe that I belong in this space too. I now travel weekends solo with confidence and joy.

Emma, 32 – Groningen, NL

So many 'what ifs' held me back from vanlife. Campher made me feel safe and showed me how to prepare. With guides, legal parking info, and emergency prep tips, I took two solo trips and loved every second. I now travel smarter and happier.

Tess, 30 – Nijmegen, NL

Campher helped me turn 'what ifs' into 'I've got this.' I learned everything from where to park to how to mentally prep for a solo trip. I’ve now explored the coast and the Veluwe solo. Their support helped me live my dream, safely and fully.
