Safety Tips

Explore our top safety tips for solo campers

Choose Safe Locations

Stay in well-lit and populated campgrounds with visible security features like gates or surveillance. Always check recent reviews and avoid secluded spots when alone. Try to stick to places close to help services in case of emergency.

Create the Illusion of Company

Leave large shoes outside or place a mannequin inside your van to appear accompanied. Display tools or heavy gear to suggest someone strong is nearby. Avoid leaving weapons easily accessible as it may backfire.

Connect with Fellow Campers

Befriend nearby campers. Friendly neighbors can be extra eyes and support when needed. Even better, you can make friends for life.

Secure Your Belongings

Keep valuables out of sight and ensure your van has additional locks. Park strategically to discourage tampering.

Prepare for Wildlife

In remote areas, research local wildlife. Store food securely and stay aware of your surroundings beyond people.

Pack Essentials

Carry pepper spray, flashlight, first-aid kit, and emergency contacts. These basics go a long way in giving you peace of mind.
